Rules for the Fixture Creation in Python:

Double Round-Robin League System:

Each team will play against every other team twice: once at their home and once away.
Match Representation:

The left side of the fixture represents the home team, and the right side represents the away team.
Odd Number of Teams:

If the number of teams entered is odd, an additional team named "Bay" should be added to make the total number of teams even.
Teams matched with "Bay" in a given week will not play that week.
Even Number of Teams Scenario:

If the number of teams is already even, no "Bay" team is added.
All teams will play against each other according to the rules of a double round-robin system.
